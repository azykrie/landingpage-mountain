About Us

Discover the Majesty of Mount Rinjani

Mount Rinjani, towering at 3,726 meters, is the second-highest volcano in Indonesia. Located on the island of Lombok, this majestic peak offers adventurers stunning views, lush forests, and the breathtaking Segara Anak crater lake.

Trekkers from around the world are drawn to Rinjani's summit for the challenge, beauty, and spiritual significance. Whether you're chasing sunrise at the peak or relaxing by the crater, Rinjani is an unforgettable journey.
